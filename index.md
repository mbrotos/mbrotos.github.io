## About me

I currently pursue a thesis-based MSc in Computer Science (AI) at Toronto Metropolitan University as a Vector Institute Scholar, with experience spanning industry, healthcare, public sector, and research labs. My work combines deep learning, reinforcement learning, and probabilistic modeling to solve real-world problems under practical constraints such as data sparsity, privacy, and measurable business impact.
